About Our Antenatal Care

Our Antenatal Care (ANC) Unit provides specialized care for expectant mothers throughout their pregnancy journey. We offer evidence-based medical care combined with compassionate support to ensure the health and well-being of both mother and baby.

First Trimester Care

Early pregnancy assessments, dating scans, and screening for potential complications during the critical first 12 weeks.

Second Trimester Care

Detailed anomaly scans, fetal growth monitoring, and preparation for the coming months of pregnancy.

Third Trimester Care

Preparation for delivery, fetal positioning checks, and monitoring for late-pregnancy complications.

Pregnancy Warning Signs

Seek immediate medical attention if you experience: severe abdominal pain, vaginal bleeding, sudden swelling, severe headaches, or decreased fetal movement.
